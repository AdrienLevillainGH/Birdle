How to Play BirdL

BirdL is a daily bird guessing game inspired by Wordle, similar to 'Birdle' or 'Birdie'. Each day, one mystery bird is selected from a curated global dataset. You have 10 guesses to find it using ecological and biological hints revealed after each attempt.

Game objective

Your goal is to identify the mystery bird in 10 attempts. BirdL is designed for both novice and experienced bird enthusiasts, encouraging exploration and discovery rather than penalizing mistakes.

How guessing works

After each guess, BirdL reveals clues about the mystery bird’s characteristics, such as:

  • Habitat
  • Diet
  • Migration strategy
  • Taxonomy and morphology

Each new guess helps you narrow down the possible species. There are no wrong answers — only birds to discover.

Daily puzzle

BirdL resets every day at UTC midnight. A new mystery bird is available daily, giving players a fresh challenge every 24 hours.
